The Vision

Two founders. One shared obsession.

Adrian and Constantin — two founders, one shared obsession. After years of witnessing how extraordinary travel experiences were locked behind absurd price tags, they decided to shatter the status quo.

Project Lazarus was born from a radical idea: that everyone deserves to live unforgettable moments without emptying their bank account.
